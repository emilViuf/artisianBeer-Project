About us

3wizards is an artisanal brewery based in the Copenhagen area. Our goal is to challenge and push the boundaries of conventional beer making and tasting, with our very own line of artisanal beers.

We dedicated ourselves to offering customers a unique experience with every sip. Our beers are brewed with precision, passion and compassion for the environment. We strive for our products to be of the highest quality on the market, rich in taste, and sustainable.

No need to go to overpriced restaurants or bars in order to enjoy quality beer. Thanks to our delivery services, you can now enjoy high-quality, sustainable beers, right from the comfort of your home. Just lay back and savour them.

Our services can be acquired by signing up for a monthly subscription.
